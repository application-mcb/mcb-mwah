## Here is How Student Enrollment Works

### Step 1: Account Setup

Students create their profile by providing:

- Personal information (name, birth date, contact details)
- Family background
- Address information

### Step 2: Choose Your Program

Students select what they want to study:

- **High School**: Pick between Junior High (Grades 7-10) or Senior High (Grades 11-12)
- **College**: Choose a specific course like Information Technology, Business Administration, etc.

### Step 3: Upload Documents

Students upload important papers like:

- Birth certificate
- Report cards
- Medical certificates
- ID pictures

### Step 4: Select Subjects

Based on their chosen program, students get a list of subjects they need to take for that school year.

### Step 5: Get Section Assignment

After enrollment is approved, students are placed in a specific class section with other students.

---

## Here is How Courses Are Created

### Step 1: Program Setup

Registrars create different academic programs:

- **College Courses**: Like "Bachelor of Science in Information Technology" (BSIT)
- **High School Grades**: Like "Grade 11 STEM" or "Grade 9 Regular"

For each program, registrars add subjects that students must take:

- **Math subjects**: Algebra, Calculus, Statistics
- **Science subjects**: Biology, Chemistry, Physics
- **Language subjects**: English, Filipino
- **Specialized subjects**: Computer Programming, Business Management

### Step 3: Subject Organization

Subjects are grouped into "Subject Sets" based on grade level and semester.

## Here is How Grade Calculation Works

### Grading System Overview

The system uses a comprehensive grading structure:

#### Grading Periods

- **4 Grading Periods** per academic year
- **College**: First Semester (2 periods), Second Semester (2 periods)
- **Senior High School**: Same as college
- **Junior High School**: Continuous 4 periods

#### Grade Components

**Academic Grades (1-100 scale)**

- **Period 1-4**: Individual grading period scores
- **Final Grade**: Calculated average of all periods

**Special Grade Statuses**

- **INC**: Incomplete (missing requirements)
- **FA**: Failed (below passing grade)
- **FW**: Failed due to withdrawal
- **W**: Withdrawn from subject

#### Grade Categories

**Regular Subjects**

- Mathematics, Science, English, Filipino
- Social Studies, Values Education
- Physical Education, Health

**Specialized Subjects (College/SHS)**

- Computer Programming, Business subjects
- STEM subjects, Arts subjects
- Technical vocational subjects

### Grade Entry Process

#### For Teachers

1. **Select Subject & Section**: Choose the class to grade
2. **View Student List**: See all enrolled students
3. **Enter Period Grades**: Input grades for each grading period
4. **Add Special Status**: Mark INC, FA, FW, or W when needed
5. **Save Grades**: Automatically updates student records

#### Grade Validation

- **Range Check**: Grades must be 1-100 or valid status
- **Required Fields**: All periods must be completed
- **Audit Trail**: System tracks who entered grades and when
